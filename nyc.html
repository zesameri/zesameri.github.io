Where to stay

Williamsburg
Neighborhood
Gentrified but convenient — easy access into Manhattan or the rest of Brooklyn. Great base if the wedding is anywhere central.
Greenpoint
Neighborhood
North of Williamsburg, less convenient for Manhattan but full of culture and happenings. Quiet evenings — Citi Bike/Uber/metro to the party parts of Brooklyn.
Downtown Brooklyn / Brooklyn Heights
Neighborhood
Stroller city in the day but easy to get everywhere from here. The oldest part of Brooklyn — lots of charm and prewar architecture.
